Features

Designed for Japanese learners

Every detail built around how you actually study — not around how a product manager thinks you study.

🎯

JLPT N5–N3 Precision

Vocabulary is classified into exactly the levels you need for exam prep. No N1 noise, no beginner-only limitations — just the sweet spot.

⏱️

Timestamp Deep Links

Every flashcard links to the precise second in the YouTube video where the word appears. Hear native pronunciation in real context, instantly.

🔄

Deduplicated & Sorted

Repeated words are merged with occurrence counts. Sort by frequency, JLPT level, or study however you like. No manual cleanup needed.

🚫

No Login. No Database.

Everything runs locally in your browser. No accounts, no tracking, no server. Your study data is yours — it disappears when you close the tab.

🎨

JLPT Color Coding

N5 green, N4 orange, N3 red — consistent visual language across the entire experience. Instantly know the difficulty of any word at a glance.

⌨️

Keyboard-First Navigation

Arrow keys to move between cards, Space or Enter to flip. Built for people who want to study fast without touching a mouse.
